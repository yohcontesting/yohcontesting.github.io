About Dr. Bernard Kadio - Board Member

Dr. Bernard Kadio, MD, MPH, PhD, not only holds the distinguished position of public and global health professor at Bob Jones University but is also the visionary force behind transformative initiatives. As the Center for Community and Global Health director and the public health/global health program, his extensive expertise spans international health research, clinical care (focusing on mental health and neurological disorders in infants and children), and impactful involvement in medical missions.

Beyond the classroom, Dr. Kadio is actively engaged in Church planting and empowering vulnerable groups from a Christian perspective through medical missions. Currently, he's leading a dedicated team of Christian health professionals in establishing the Hope Christian Health Center in the Ivory Coast, his home country. This cutting-edge ministry seamlessly integrates clinical care, public health, and medical evangelism, marking a groundbreaking endeavor.

On a personal note, Dr. Kadio shares his life journey with his wife, Dr. Nathalie Kadio, MD, ENT, and their son, Johanne Kadio. Join us in celebrating a leader whose commitment to health and humanity leaves an indelible mark on both local and global scales.
