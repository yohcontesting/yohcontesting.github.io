Walking Together: Join Our Mission to Support Orthopedic Care in South Africa

Embark on a journey of compassion with us! This time, our mission takes us to the heart of South Africa, specifically to the children in need of orthotics and prosthetics. Miraculously, we've connected with Phekolong Hospital in Bethlehem, just a scenic 3-hour drive from Johannesburg. This town is home to many children and adults grappling with challenges post-polio or limb amputation and those born with conditions like clubfoot or cerebral palsy, all in dire need of orthotics.

In Bethlehem, prayers for assistance have echoed, and we're the answer. Gratitude fills our hearts for the local clinics rallying behind this cause. Together, we are collecting orthotics, prosthetics, and shoes. Your financial contribution can transform lives and make a lasting impact. Join us on this extraordinary mission, and let's be the change-makers South Africa is praying for. Together, we can turn prayers into tangible blessings.
