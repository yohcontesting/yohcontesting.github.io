About Our Team

Meet Our Physician

Dr. Sandra E. Luyindula

CEO & Founder

Biography

Dr. Luyindula earned her Bachelor's in Biology Premed with double minors in Chemistry and French from Xavier University of Louisiana. She then attended Ross University School of Medicine. She completed her family medicine residency at Greenville Health System, where she received the Greenville Health System Gold Award.

Dr. Luyindula is triple board-certified in Family Medicine, Obesity Medicine and Lifestyle Medicine. She enjoys providing care for patients of all ages and making house calls for her patients. She has a particular interest in women's health and aesthetics medicine. What she loves about practicing medicine is her connection with her patients and the ability to cheer them on as they work to achieve their various goals. She strongly believes that for her, medicine is ministry and ministry is medicine.

When not in medicine, you will find her spending time with her dear husband and 3 children, in her kitchen creating new recipes, or traveling.

Fun fact: She speaks multiple languages, she can venture into up to 10 languages.
