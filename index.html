Welcome to The Well Projects

Who Are We?

At The Well Projects, we're a 501(c)(3) - nonprofit organization on a mission fueled by love and driven by impact. Inspired by Matthew 25:35-36, our goal is clear: to be the tangible expression of God's love in a world that needs it.

Matthew 25: 35-36: “for I was hungry and you gave Me food; I was thirsty and you gave Me drink; I was a stranger and you took Me in; I was naked and you clothed Me; I was sick and you visited Me; I was in prison and you came to Me.”
