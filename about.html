Our History

The Well Projects is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization founded in 2023, fueled by love and driven by impact. Inspired by the words of Jesus in Matthew 25:35-36 — "For I was hungry and you gave me food, I was thirsty and you gave me drink..." — we exist to be a tangible expression of God's love in a world that deeply needs it.

Born from a calling placed on our hearts and launched through a mission opportunity in South Africa, The Well Projects now reaches across the street and across the globe. Whether it's providing food to a hungry family, supporting orphaned children, offering prosthetic care to someone affected by polio or cerebral palsy, meeting urgent medical needs, or simply clothing the vulnerable—our mission is to meet basic human needs while spreading the hope of the gospel.

We serve locally, nationally, and internationally, not confined by borders but guided by faith. Wherever God sends us, we go.

As a faith-based organization, we believe ministry begins with compassion and continues through action. Every donation, every prayer, every act of kindness helps us carry the living water of Christ to dry and hurting places.
