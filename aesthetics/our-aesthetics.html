Frequently Asked Questions

When do I need to decide to start getting Botox?

It is a personal decision and can depend on various factors, such as genetics, lifestyle choices, and individual preferences. However, most people who get Botox injections for cosmetic purposes typically start in their 30s or 40s when they notice the first signs of aging, such as fine lines and wrinkles.

When is the best time to start getting Botox?

The best time is before wrinkles become deeply etched into the skin. It is more effective to prevent wrinkles from forming than to reverse the existing ones - preventive. You can also prevent deep lines from forming, hence a more youthful appearance over time.

How long do Botox injections last?

Smooth out wrinkles with Botox—results last 3-6 months. Keep the effects with regular injections. Get personalized advice from our experts, discover the benefits, and ask any questions you have.
