Chemical Peels

Chemical Peels at The WellMD: Refine and Reveal

At The WellMD, our chemical peels are more than a skincare treatment - they're a deeply renewing experience for your skin and your confidence. Thoughtfully selected and medically guided, our peels are designed to gently exfoliate, brighten and rebalance your complexion from the inside out.

Each peel is performed by our expert aesthetician - who brings over a decade of hands-on experience - and is tailored under physician supervision to meet your skin's unique needs.

Talk To Us

Why Choose a Chemical Peel?

Chemical peels accelerate cell turnover and collagen production by removing dull, damaged layers of skin - revealing the fresher, clearer skin beneath. Whether you're seeking a radiant glow for a special occasion or long-term correction, peels are a time-tested, science-backed way to elevate your skin health.

What We Treat

Our chemical peels address a wide range of concerns, including:

  • Acne & post-inflammatory discoloration.
  • Fine lines & early signs of aging.
  • Melasma & hyperpigmentation.
  • Uneven skin tone & texture.
  • Sun damage.
  • Dull or tired-looking skin.

We also offer enzyme peels for a gentler touch - perfect for sensitive skin or first-time guests.

Your Skin, Tailored Treatment

At The Well MD, no two peels are alike. After a comprehensive skin analysis, we'll select the peel best suited to your skin goals and sensitivities. Our peels range in strength from mild to medium-depth and are chosen from respected, medical-grade lines known for safety and effectiveness.

The Experience

Each chemical peel session is about 45-60 minutes of focused care in a peaceful, restorative setting. You'll leave with glowing skin and a personalized post-care plan to extend your results. Most clients experience light flaking over a few days, followed by a noticeably smoother, more luminous complexion. At The Well MD, we believe radiant skin is part of whole-body wellness - and we're honored to walk that journey with you.

Frequently Asked Questions: Chemical Peels at The WellMD

What skin concerns can chemical peels help with?

Chemical peels can improve:

  • Acne and acne scars.
  • Hyperpigmentation and melasma.
  • Sun damage.
  • Uneven skin tone & texture.
  • Fine lines and dullness.

We also offer gentle enzyme peels for more sensitive skin or first-time clients.

Do chemical peels hurt?

Most clients describe the sensation as mild tingling or warmth during the peel. You may feel a bit of tightness or light flaking afterward, depending on the depth of the peel. We ensure your comfort throughout the entire process.

How long is the recovery time?

Recovery depends on the depth of the peel. For light to medium peels, you may notice some dryness or peeling for 3-7 days. During this time, we recommend avoiding direct sun exposure and using gentle, hydrating skincare as advised by your aesthetician.

How many treatments will I need?

You'll notice improvement after just one session, but for optimal results, we typically recommend a series of peels. Your aesthetician will help you create a personalized treatment plan based on your skin goals.

Can I combine a chemical peel with other treatments?

Yes! Many clients combine peels with facials or dermaplaning. We'll guide you on safe and effective combinations for your skin.

Is a chemical peel right for my skin type?

Most skin types benefit from chemical peels, but we always begin with a detailed consultation to evaluate your skin, lifestyle and sensitivities. We also offer alternatives like enzyme peels for those who need a gentler approach.

What should I do before my appointment?

You'll receive personalized pre-treatment instructions, including which products (like retinoids or exfoliants) to pause a few days before your peel. Avoiding sun exposure and staying well-hydrated will also prepare your skin for the best possible results.

How should I care for my skin after a peel?

After your peel, we recommend:

  • Avoiding exfoliants and active skincare for 5-7 days.
  • Applying gentle moisturizers and SPF daily.
  • Not picking or peeling flaking skin.
  • Staying out of direct sunlight and heat.

You'll receive customized aftercare instructions at your visit to help your skin heal beautifully.

Do you offer packages or memberships?

Yes! We offer treatment bundles at a discounted rate and our Direct Primary Care (DPC) members enjoy exclusive pricing on all aesthetic services.
