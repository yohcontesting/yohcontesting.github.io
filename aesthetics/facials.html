Facials

Where Skin Health Meets Luxury Wellness

At The WellMD, we believe that true wellness includes the health of your skin. As part of our comprehensive, whole-person approach to care, we are proud to offer a curated menu of professional facial treatments - designed to help you look and feel your best from the inside out.

Our facials are more than skin-deep - they're an experience. Under the guidance of our physician and performed by a certified esthetician with over 10 years of expertise, each treatment is tailored to your unique skin type and goals, using only the highest-quality medical-grade products and techniques.

Our Facial Treatments

Whether you're seeking hydration, rejuvenation, or a deep cleanse, we offer a full range of customized services to meet your skincare needs:

  • More than Classic Facial: A luxurious deep-cleansing treatment that leaves your skin refreshed, radiant and nourished.
  • Dermaplaning Facial: Removes dead skin and peach fuzz for a smoother, brighter complexion and better product absorption.
  • Acne Facial: Targeted extractions, calming treatments, and medical-grade products to clarify and calm breakout-prone skin.
  • Anti-Aging Facial: Focuses on reducing fine lines, improving skin tone and elasticity and enhancing your skin's natural glow.
  • Hydration Facial: Replenishes moisture and restores balance - perfect for dry, tired or travel-worn skin.
  • Chemical Peels & Enzyme Peels: Gently exfoliate and resurface the skin for a brighter, smoother and more even complexion.

Enhance Your Experience
We offer a range of add-ons, such as specialty masks, dermaplaning, targeted serums and extractions, to personalize and elevate your treatment.

Why Choose The Well MD for Facials:

  • Medical-Grade Skincare in a calming boutique setting.
  • Experienced Aesthetician with a decade of trusted hands.
  • Physician-Supervised Treatments for your peace of mind.
  • Customized for Your Skin Type and wellness goals.
  • Exclusive Discounts for DPC Members.
  • Facial Packages & Bundles available.

What to Expect:

Each facial lasts approximately 60 minutes, with personalized prep and aftercare instructions provided. Our team is here to ensure you feel supported every step of the way - from the moment you walk in to the glow you leave with.

We can't wait to help you experience the healing power of touch, skin-deep confidence and radiant wellness - because you deserve to feel well in every way.
