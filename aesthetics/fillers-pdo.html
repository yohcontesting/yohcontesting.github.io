Fillers | PDO

The Well MD now introduces PDO threading!

Experience the “thread facelift” or “thread lifting”, a procedure designed to lift and tighten sagging skin on your face and neck. Made from Polydioxanone (PDO), an absorbable and biocompatible material, PDO threading offers immediate support to sagging areas. Over time, it stimulates collagen production, enhancing your skin's firmness for a rejuvenated look.

The Advantages of PDO Threading:

  • Experience immediate lifting and support, with enhanced results over time.
  • Threads naturally dissolve over several months.
  • Continuous collagen production for lasting skin improvement.
  • A non-invasive alternative to surgical lifts.
  • Minimal downtime for your convenience.
  • Results can last from 6 to 24 months.

Dermal Fillers:

Dermal fillers are commonly used to restore lost volume, provide a more youthful appearance, enhance facial features, and reduce the appearance of wrinkles, fine lines, and other signs of aging. There are many types; the most common, used at our office, is Hyaluronic Acid Fillers (HA). HA is a substance naturally found in the skin. As we age, there is a natural decrease in the production of hyaluronic acid and an increase in its degradation, contributing to the aging process. Some examples include Juvéderm and Restylane. Hyaluronic acid fillers typically last 6-18 months, while others may have longer-lasting effects.

Like any cosmetic procedure, licensed professionals should only perform PDO threading in a safe and sterile environment. And most of all, it should be personalized and individualized.
