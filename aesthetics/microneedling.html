Microneedling at The Well MD : Where Science Meets Skin Confidence

At The Well MD, our approach to microneedling blends the best of natural skin renewal with the precision of medical-grade technology. Performed by our skilled aesthetician with over a decade of experience - and always under the supervision of our physician - each session is thoughtfully designed to reveal brighter, smoother and more resilient skin.

What Microneedling Can Do for You:

Whether you're looking to soften fine lines, fade acne scars or brighten stubborn hyperpigmentation, microneedling helps awaken your skin's natural healing response by stimulating collagen and elastin. The result? A radiant complexion with lasting improvements in tone, texture and clarity.

What We Treat:

  • Fine lines & early signs of aging.
  • Acne scarring & uneven skin texture.
  • Hyperpigmentation & sun damage.
  • Skin laxity.
  • Overall dullness and tone.

Microneedling isn't just for the face - we could also treat the neck, décolletage and even the scalp.

Treatment Plan:

We typically recommend a bundle of three sessions, spaced 4-6 weeks apart, followed by maintenance to keep your glow going strong. Each session includes a personalized plan based on your skin's unique needs.

Natural Beauty, Elevated by Science

We believe in enhancing your skin's natural beauty. Our treatments combine holistic care with medical-grade tools to deliver results that feel as good as they look. You'll leave with a refreshed complexion - and the confidence that comes with it.

Your aesthetician will provide personalized aftercare instructions at the end of your visit to help you heal beautifully and protect your results. Important pre-treatment guidelines, such as pausing certain skincare products, will be shared with you upon booking.

Frequently Asked Questions: Microneedling at The WellMD

Is microneedling uncomfortable?

Your comfort is our priority. We begin each session with a soothing, medical-grade numbing cream to ensure a virtually painless experience. Most guests describe the sensation as a gentle buzzing or light prickling.

How much downtime should I expect?

Expect a healthy post-glow. Your skin may appear pink and feel sun-kissed for 24-48 hours. With proper aftercare, most clients resume their usual activities the very next day - radiating confidence.

When will I begin to see results?

Many begin to notice a refreshed, radiant glow within the first week. Deeper improvements in texture, tone and firmness unfold gradually over 3-6 weeks, with results that build beautifully over time.

Can I wear makeup after my appointment?

We recommend allowing your skin to breathe for 24 hours post-treatment. After that, clean, non-comedogenic makeup can be applied as needed - though many find they need far less.

Is microneedling suitable for all skin types?

Absolutely. Our approach is customized and inclusive, with techniques that are safe and effective for all complexions, including melanin-rich skin.

What makes microneedling at The WellMD different?

Every detail is elevated - from our esthetician's skilled hands and clinical expertise to our serene environment and physician-guided protocols. This isn't just a treatment - it's a journey of restoration.
