Why Donate? Because Love in Action Changes Everything.

Together, we can make the world a better place—one act of kindness, one donation at a time. Be part of something bigger; be part of The Well Projects.

You can donate with PayPal to info@thewellprojects.org or by clicking the PayPal button below

You can also donate with Zelle to info@thewellprojects.org or by scanning the QR code

  1. Find Zelle® in your banking app, click “Send,” then click on the QR code icon displayed at the top of the “Select Recipient” screen.
  2. Your phone's camera will open.
  3. To send money using a Zelle® QR code, simply point your camera at the recipient's Zelle® QR code, enter the amount, hit “Send,” and the money is on the way!
